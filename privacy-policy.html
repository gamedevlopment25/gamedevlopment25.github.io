This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile game application (the “App”).

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, precise location, or contacts.

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google Inc., to display advertisements, including interstitial and rewarded ads.

Google AdMob may collect non-personal information such as device information, advertising identifiers, and general usage data to serve ads. These ads are configured to be non-personalised and family-safe in compliance with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Learn more about how Google handles data:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We are committed to protecting children’s privacy. The App is suitable for all ages and complies with the Google Play Families Policy.

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of personal data being stored or misused by us. Third-party services such as Google AdMob operate under their own privacy and security practices.
