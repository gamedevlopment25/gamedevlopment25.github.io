About the Game

Rescue Driver is a scenic driving game where skill, focus, and calm decision-making matter. A group of people is trapped on a mountain top due to heavy snowfall and vehicle breakdown. You play as a driver on a rescue journey through difficult terrain to reach them and bring them safely home.

Gameplay Features

  • Drive through roads, slopes, mountains, and tunnels
  • Peaceful scenic environments
  • Avoid collisions with trucks, cars, and tractors
  • Smooth and simple controls
  • Family-friendly, non-violent gameplay
  • Offline playable
